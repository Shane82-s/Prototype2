Welcome

This platform provides mental health and psychological support for students in higher education. Explore resources, connect with counselors, and take care of your well-being.

Stress-Relieving Tips

  • 🌿 Take 5–10 minute breaks every hour to recharge.
  • 🧘 Practice deep breathing or short meditation sessions.
  • 🚶 Go for a walk outside to clear your mind.
  • 📱 Limit screen time before bed to improve sleep quality.
  • 💬 Talk to friends, family, or a counselor when feeling overwhelmed.

Study Tips for Success

  • 📅 Use a planner to organize tasks and avoid last-minute cramming.
  • ⏳ Break study time into focused 25–30 minute sessions (Pomodoro method).
  • ✍️ Take handwritten notes to improve memory retention.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated and eat brain-boosting foods.
  • 😴 Get enough sleep — a well-rested mind learns faster.

Resources

  • Mindfulness Exercises – Simple breathing and meditation techniques for daily practice.
  • Support Groups – Connect with peers who understand what you’re going through.
